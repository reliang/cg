Monster Express

2D Game

2020

Unity

A friend and I created this memory-based pixel game on Unity after being inspired by Studio Ghibli's movie, "Spirited Away." In the movie, a scene depicted a train carrying passengers through a beautiful endless body of water. According to my friend, the train scene represents life; in life, people come and go, just as how the spirits on the train came and left at different stops.

For this first level of the game, we created our own game characters, character animation, and background. We coded in game functionality using C#, and I also designed and developed the UI. For the future, we might develop more levels as well as a clearer storyline.

My Weird Neighbor

2D Game

2020

Unity

This level-based puzzle game that I created with a friend was inspired by my co-living experience. In this game, the objective is to piece apartment blocks together based on people's needs, just as how roommates in the same apartment ideally have fitting personalities and habits.

We have developed 4 different levels so far. In the process, We created game assets, puzzle logic, and level select functionality. I also designed and developed the UI based on usability and space constraints.

Crossy Roadkill

3D Game

2020

Unreal Engine 4

You are driving down a three-lane highway through a forest when you realize there are animals crossing the road. And not just one, but myriad, chickens, deers, and more. This is a game that allows you to be on the driver, or antagonist, side of Crossy Road. Your goal is to hit as many animals as possible as the antagonistic player.

Created in a team of 3 for game design class, I was responsible for writing the high concept, the design document, designing the UI, and creating blueprints for the game mechanism and UI.

Character Project

3D Model

2018

Maya

My goal for this project was to experiment with character modeling in Maya. I was inspired by the Instagram artist @daisukerichard. Most of his characters wear loose jackets, carry backpacks, have a bob hairstyle, and have obscured faces. In many side profiles he draws, only a nose could be seen. That is why I decided to do a character with no facial features besides a nose.

I was also inspired by the video “K/DA - POP/STARS,” which has a sharp, constrasting look to each scene, and employs polygon forms to create the hair. I strived to make my model with an edge as well. That is why I chose to keep my model low-poly, with certain unsmoothed surfaces.

Pursuit of Happiness

2D Animation

2019

Toon Boom

Created in the span of three months, The Pursuit of Happiness is a hand-drawn semi-surreal animation. In the beginning, the character hopes to find happiness by endlessly chasing an unreachable image of success, but by the end, she realizes that contentment can lie in the smaller things. I want the scenes to transition in a fluid, creative way. Scenes I envisioned include calendar dates collapsing into each other, and the character chasing herself.

Playboy Bunny

3D Model & Rig

2019

Maya

For this project, I strived to further improve my character design and modeling skills, as well as understand the rigging process. Therefore, I set the goal of creating a walk cycle for my character.

This character idea came from the desire to design an abstract and cartoony character with exaggerated features, such as elongated legs and arms. The persona I wanted to convey was a serious and somewhat bossy character, therefore, the walk cycle involved the bunny taking long, confident strides.

In the future, I want to experiment with rigging more exaggerated movements in order to make my characters more expressive.

2D Animation

2019

Photoshop

Food sustainability was the theme for Penn Create’s 2019 gallery show. As a member of the art club Penn Create, I also created an artpiece to be displayed. Japanese Torii Gate

Real-Time Rendering

2020

WebGL

View the full shader and code on ShaderToy. This is the final project of my Advanced Computer Graphics course. The animated scene, completely rendered through WebGL code, was inspired by the Japanese Torii Gate—a red, iconic gate that usually stands in the entrance of a Shinto shrine. The scene incorporates procedural texturing within the mountains, grass, water, and roof, as well as features such as specular reflection, Blinn-Phong reflection, depth of field, ambient occlusion, and the smooth-blending of SDFs.

Physically-Based Rendering

Graphics Rendering

2020

C++, Qt

I rendered these scenes to demonstrate the global illumination capabilities of the Full Lighting Integrator, after implementing it as part of the Advanced Computer Graphics course. The integrator combines Naive integration with Direct Lighting (involving Multiple-Importance Sampling). This captures both direct and indirect light, as well as reflections from materials like glass. The first scene involves orbs with glass materials of various indices of reflection and a microfacet mirror. The second scene demonstrates orbs with microfacet mirror materials of varying roughness. The last scene shows different geometries with plastic and glass materials, as well as microfacet mirrors with varying roughness in the back.
